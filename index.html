Pas Luna

Pas Luna is a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting students through scholarship opportunities, community care, and educational access. Rooted in legacy, compassion, and the belief that students deserve meaningful support, Pas Luna works to create opportunities for those pursuing their academic and personal goals.

Through scholarships and community-centered programming, Pas Luna seeks to honor the stories, families, and futures that shape students’ journeys. Our mission is to make educational support more accessible, personal, and empowering.
